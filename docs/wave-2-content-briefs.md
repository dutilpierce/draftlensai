## Not planned (anti-spam)

- 50-city “AI proofreading in `<city>`” pages.
- Auto-translated clones of English pages without human QA.
- Thin `/blog/` tags or author pages for nonexistent writers.

## Brief template (for each Wave-2 page)

1. **Query & intent** — What does the searcher need in the first 120 words?
2. **DraftLens tie-in** — One paragraph on how the product behaves (no exaggeration).
3. **Evidence** — Links to `/methodology`, relevant feature pages, or external primary sources.
4. **CTA** — `/app` for trial; `/contact` for enterprise questions.
5. **Schema** — Usually `Article` + `BreadcrumbList`; no FAQ schema unless UI shows a real FAQ.
